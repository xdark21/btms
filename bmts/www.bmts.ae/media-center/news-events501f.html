Bollywood Parks equipped with industrial and life safety solutions by BMTS

Dubai -November 2016: Bahri & Mazroei Technical Systems (BMTS), the leading provider of solutions to building, industrial automation and infra-development segments in the UAE, has completed one of its largest project in industrial solutions for the recently opened Bollywood Parks, a Dubai Parks & Resorts projects. BMTS provided industrial and life safety solutions to the world's first theme park, which is dedicated to the Bollywood film industry.
As an end-to-end technology and managed services company in the areas of safety, security, power, audio visual and building automation, BMTS is associated with major projects in the UAE in various capacities with its IoT and Machine to Machine (M2M) based software solutions. For the project BMTS has closely worked with trusted partners that included two consultants and two different MEP Contractors. The complex project was conducted for 12 buildings and the magnanimous RajMahal Theatre.
Commenting on the project, Mr. Esam Al Mazroei, Vice Chairman of Bahri & Mazroei Group, said, "We are so proud that we could successfully complete the milestone project for the one-of-its-kind Bollywood Park in Dubai. We are proud to be associated with this as no other theme park has been entirely dedicated to one of the biggest film industries in the world. This has been one of our unique projects, but the team at BMTS could ensure everything going ahead perfectly."
"We have implemented the most advanced technologies for this project and the result has been phenomenal and overwhelming. Our solution for the RajMahal not only ensures safety, but also is entirely non-intrusive on the space. Every life safety system at the Park has been supplied, installed, tested and commissioned by BMTS," he added.

Further highlighting BMTS' role in the project, Mr. Sivakumar Subramanian, General Manager of BMTS, said, "BMTS was assigned with the installation of industrial and life safety solutions for the Bollywood Parks project. There is also a system in place where in we have installed a complex monitoring system that connects all buildings. We have used the best suitable and top functional products and technology for this project. From understanding the specifics to choosing the right equipment, installing and testing them, we had our hands full for months." "We are indeed thankful to Meraas for choosing our services for the Bollywood Parks project," he added.The theme park is equipped with state-of-the-art district cooling systems from Tabreed with all structures connected to the district cooling package pipelines. The control, measurement, supply of chilled water supply to the cooling system is regulated by state of the industrial automation system comprising RTUs, programmable field measurement, programmable logic controllers to ensure the optimization of chilled water quantity and energy for pumping the same.The RajMahal theatre at Bollywood Parks proved to be a challenge for BMTS safety professionals. The team had to consider plays, acts and sequences that would make use of balloons and celebratory fires which could trigger false alarms. The intelligent safety systems offered by BMTS are superior in technology and differentiate the usage from real hazard which may occur. BMTS has used a solution called OSID technology for this purpose.

BMTS implements a range of innovative and smart solutions for monitoring and controlling numerous buildings in the UAE and beyond. BMTS provides the right combination of energy efficient design with high-end solutions in areas like HVAC, lighting, energy, water management, and renewable power generation applications.

BMTS also provides advanced technological solutions for power management like maintaining quality, controlling power and monitoring equipment; ICT-based web or cloud energy management software, big data analytics and IoT solutions. These are in combination with various energy saving service solutions, and energy performance contracting services that can optimize both capital and operational expenditures without compromising on quality.

Samsung honors BMTS with Platinum Partner award

Dubai - February 2016: Bahri & Mazroei Technical Systems (BMTS) has been honored with the Platinum Partner award by Samsung at the recently held Samsung event. The award was received by Mr. Ali Al Mazroei, Chief Financial Officer of Bahri & Mazroei Group in the presence of officials from Samsung and other security system integrators in the region. The award comes as a testimony to the team of BMTS and the company's market share in the security systems industry.BMTS has been actively representing Samsung since 2014 in CCTV and access control systems. The association and volume of business between the two companies has been increasing over the years.

With the growth in the real estate sector, system integrations and security systems have gained high prominence in the region. BMTS' market share in the UAE in the security systems integration market reaches 15 per cent. Commercial buildings, residential properties, shopping malls, hospitals, parks, museums, hotels, industrial facilities among others form the client base for BMTS.

Commenting on the honour, Mr. Esam R. Al Mazroei, Managing Director of Bahri & Mazroei Group said "We are very proud to be associated with Samsung and to be honoured by Samsung is a boost to us and a testimony to our hard work and efforts".

"The security industry in the UAE is booming and our market share is seeing a steep rise in the demand for such services. We are well prepared to provide services that include CCTV, access control, intrusion alarm systems, pedestrian and vehicle barriers. It is an absolute honor to represent Samsung in this region. The UAE government has been addressing and creating necessary steps to ensure security threats are addressed seriously and BMTS is at the forefront of providing custom made solutions for security across various needs".

BMTS has been the frontrunner and leading systems' integrator with solutions in the areas of Fire Detection and Alarm Systems, Central Battery Emergency Lighting Systems, Public Address, Voice Alarm Systems, Building Management, Security and Access Control, Intelligent Lighting Controls, Power Solutions, Home Automation Solutions, Audio-Visual Solutions and Facilities Management Systems for different building and infrastructure categories in the region.

BMTS has been associated with major projects in the UAE in various installation capacities. Some projects undertaken by BMTS include Dubai Police Forensic Laboratories, IMG Theme Park, Etihad Museum, Dubai Trade Centre District, Al Amal Psychiatric Hospital, Viceroy Hotels and Resorts.

 

BMTS wins appreciation for successfully handing over ‘The One Tower’

BMTS – the regions' leading Systems Integrator recently completed the successful supply, testing and commissioning of a CEAG Central Battery System at the prestigious One Tower in Dubai. The One Tower is a prestigious commercial tower rising up 47 floors – it has a distinctive architectural design that reflects the design aspirations of the master planners of the TECOM Zone C District. The One Tower showcases a dynamic mix of functions displayed in a simple and clean architectural design.

The MEP Contractor on the project M/s. Al Shafar United for Electro-Mechanical Engineering LLC had only words of praise for the team involved with the project.

"The endless hours that they have spent working on this project and professionalism that they have portrayed have impressed the team immensely. While having such a busy schedule, it is really commendable that they have supported us to complete the project within the time. I also feel proud to be linked with them in the process and feel a great sense of achievement. We are expecting another project of this intensity and would like the same team to head it as well so that we can be sure of its success following their ability to be absolutely dedicated. Thank you once again for all your efforts and I wish BMTS a lot of success." Sarmad Najeeb – MEP Project Manager

 

Dubai Police Notification on security surveillance within a building

In an attempt to reduce crime rates across the city, the Dubai Police have taken a decision to make CCTV and Security Systems mandatory across buildings and villas across Dubai.
Bahri & Mazroei Technical Systems Co is registered with the Dubai Police to carry out these CCTV installations.
