Sheikh Khalifa Speciality Hospital
Download
Preview

The 248- bed, 699,700 sq ft Sheikh Kalifa Specialist Hospital project in Ras Al Khaimah, UAE, is expected to be the most state-of-the-art medical facility in the area designed to accommodate unique terrains, customs, culture, and history specific to the region. 

Ibn Batutta Mall
Download
Preview

The Ibn Battuta Mall is a large shopping mall on the Sheikh Zayed Road in Dubai. It is UAE’s largest theme based mall and draws inspiration from the noted Arabic traveller and explorer Ibn Battuta. The mall is split into six courts, each named after the countries and regions to which Ibn Battuta travelled.

Jumeirah Emirates Towers
Download
Preview

The Jumeirah Emirates Towers complex contains the Emirates Office Tower and the Jumeirah Emirates Towers Hotel. The two towers, whcih stand at 355 Mtrs and 309 Mtrs respectively stand as the 23rd and 47th tallest buildings in the world. The towers are connected by a 9000 Square Mtr two storey retail complex called “The Boulevard”.

Madinat Jumeirah
Download
Preview

Madinat Jumeirah is a luxurious 5 star resort spread over 40 hectares of landscapes and gardens. Designed to resemble a traditional Arabian town, the resort comprises two boutique hotels (Al Qasr and Mina A’Salam) and a courtyard of 29 summer houses called Dar Al Masyaf.
